# Lung abnormality—`OIFM_MSFT_737122`

**Synonyms:** lung lesion, pulmonary abnormality, lung pathology, airspace disease

Lung abnormalities refer to any changes or deviations from the normal structure or function of the lung tissue via chest xray. The following findings are included: generic opacity, tuberculosis, pneumonia/consolidation, nodules, atelectasis, fibrosis, lung mass, hernia, opaque hemithorax, edema, calcification.

## Attributes

### Presence—`OIFMA_MSFT_392068`

Whether lung abnormalities are visible on the imaging study.  
**Codes**: SNOMED 705057003 Presence (property) (qualifier value)  
*(Select one)*

- **absent**: Lung abnormalities are not visible.  
_RADLEX RID28473 absent; SNOMED 2667000 Absent (qualifier value)_
- **present**: Lung abnormalities are clearly visible.  
_RADLEX RID28472 present; SNOMED 52101004 Present (qualifier value)_
- **indeterminate**: Presence of lung abnormalities cannot be determined.  
_RADLEX RID39110 indeterminate; SNOMED 82334004 Indeterminate (qualifier value)_
- **unknown**: Presence of lung abnormalities is unknown.  
_RADLEX RID5655 unknown; SNOMED 261665006 Unknown (qualifier value)_
