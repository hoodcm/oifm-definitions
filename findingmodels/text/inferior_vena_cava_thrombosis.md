# Inferior vena cava thrombosis—`OIFM_OIDM_668318`

**Synonyms:** IVC thrombosis, inferior vena cava thrombus, thrombosis of the inferior vena cava, IVC thrombus

Inferior vena cava thrombosis is the formation of a thrombus within the lumen of the inferior vena cava. On cross-sectional imaging, it appears as an intraluminal filling defect and may be associated with venous dilation; this finding is exemplified by the original phrasing 'IVC thrombosis'.
