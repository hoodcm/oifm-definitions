# Sinus keros classification on ct—`OIFM_CDE_000273`

Sinus Keros Classification on CT

**Codes:** RADELEMENT RDES273 sinus keros classification on ct

## Attributes

### Keros type—`OIFMA_CDE_001790`

The Keros classification is a method of classifying the depth of the olfactory fossa on a coronal CT image through the cribiform plate.   
*(Select one)*

- **type I (0-3mm)**:  When the vertical depth of the fossa is less than 4 mm, it is designated as a Type I.    
- **type II (4-7mm)**: A Type II is 4-7 mm in depth.  
- **type III (>7mm)**: Type III is more than 7 mm  
- **indeterminate**  
- **unknown**
