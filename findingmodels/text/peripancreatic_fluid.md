# Peripancreatic fluid—`OIFM_OIDM_920919`

**Synonyms:** peripancreatic fluid, peri-pancreatic fluid, peripancreatic fluid collection, peripancreatic effusion, fluid around the pancreas

Fluid accumulation in the peripancreatic space on cross-sectional imaging, representing inflammatory exudate or fluid collection surrounding the pancreas. It is commonly associated with acute pancreatitis, pancreatic injury, postoperative leakage, or evolution into a pancreatic pseudocyst.
