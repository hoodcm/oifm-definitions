# Scoliosis—`OIFM_CDE_000051`

This module describes the common data elements for scoliosis use case.

**Codes:** RADELEMENT RDES51 scoliosis; RADLEX RID7741 spine

## Attributes

### Cobb angle—`OIFMA_CDE_000264`

Measure coronal plane deformity on anteroposterior plane radiographs in the classification of scoliosis (degrees)  
Mininum: 0  
Maximum: 100  
Unit: degrees

### Pelvic tilt—`OIFMA_CDE_000265`

Angle between the iliac crest to the horizontal (degrees)  
Mininum: 0  
Maximum: 100  
Unit: degrees

### Pelvic incidence—`OIFMA_CDE_000268`

Angle of incidence is the algebraic sum of 2 complementary angles: pelvic tilt and sacral slope (degrees)

Maximum: 100

### Risser score—`OIFMA_CDE_000273`

Grade skeletal maturity based on the level of ossification and fusion of the iliac crest apophyses  
*(Select one)*

- **stage 0**: no ossification center at the level of iliac crest apophysis  
- **stage 1**: apophysis along less than 25 percent of the iliac crest  
- **stage 2**: apophysis along 25-50 percent of the iliac crest  
- **stage 3**: apophysis along 50-75 percent of the iliac crest  
- **stage 4**: apophysis along greater than 75 percent of the iliac crest  
- **stage 5**: complete ossification and fusion of the iliac crest apophysis  
- **indeterminate**  
- **unknown**
