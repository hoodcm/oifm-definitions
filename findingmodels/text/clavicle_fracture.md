# Clavicle fracture—`OIFM_OIDM_842151`

**Synonyms:** clavicular fracture, fracture of the clavicle, clavicle fx, clavicular fx

Fracture of the clavicle with disruption of the bony cortex. It may be displaced or nondisplaced and can involve the midshaft or the medial or lateral end.
