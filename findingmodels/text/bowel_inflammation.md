# Bowel inflammation—`OIFM_OIDM_201175`

**Synonyms:** bowel inflammatory signs, bowel wall thickening, bowel wall thickening with mural enhancement, enteritis signs, colitis signs

Radiologic finding representing inflammation of the bowel; commonly demonstrated by bowel wall thickening with mural hyperenhancement and surrounding mesenteric stranding. This finding can be described as 'bowel inflammatory signs' and is seen in enteritis or colitis and may be accompanied by luminal dilation.
