# Hepatic lesion—`OIFM_OIDM_160831`

**Synonyms:** hepatic lesion, liver lesion, focal liver lesion, hepatic mass, liver mass

A hepatic lesion denotes a focal abnormality of the liver parenchyma detected on imaging. The finding encompasses a spectrum of etiologies from benign to malignant, and further characterization with dedicated liver imaging is often required to refine the diagnosis.
