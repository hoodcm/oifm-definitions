# Enostosis—`OIFM_OIDM_029466`

**Synonyms:** bone island, bone islands

Enostosis is a benign, incidental focal intramedullary sclerotic lesion arising from the endosteal surface of cancellous bone. Radiographs typically show a well-defined, homogeneously dense focus within cancellous bone without aggressive features; 'bone island' is a common radiologic synonym and represents the original descriptive phrase.
