# Chest radiograph-evident hardware—`OIFM_MSFT_623632`

**Synonyms:** chest x-ray hardware, radiopaque chest implants, thoracic implants, radiopaque chest devices

Chest radiograph-evident hardware refers to any medical device or implant within the thoracic cavity that is visible on a chest X-ray due to its radiopacity.
