# Cardiac abnormality—`OIFM_MSFT_247688`

**Synonyms:** heart abnormality, heart lesion, cardiac lesion

A cardiac abnormality refers to any structural or functional issue of the heart, which may include congenital or acquired conditions impacting cardiac performance or morphology.
