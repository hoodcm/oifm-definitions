# Parapelvic renal cyst—`OIFM_OIDM_467681`

**Synonyms:** parapelvic cyst, renal parapelvic cyst, parapelvic renal sinus cyst

Parapelvic renal cyst is a fluid-filled, non-enhancing cyst of the renal sinus adjacent to the renal pelvis. On imaging, it appears as a well-defined, simple cyst that does not communicate with the collecting system and may be incidentally encountered; large lesions may cause calyceal compression or hydronephrosis.
