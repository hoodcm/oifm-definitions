# Left ventricular assist device lvad—`OIFM_MGB_040444`

A left ventricular assist device (LVAD) is a mechanical pump that is used to support the function of the left ventricle of the heart.

## Attributes

### Presence of lvad—`OIFMA_MGB_878134`

Indicates whether an LVAD is present or absent  
*(Select one)*

- **Present**: An LVAD is present  
- **Absent**: No LVAD is present  

### Appropriate position—`OIFMA_MGB_029664`

Indicates whether the LVAD is in an appropriate position  
*(Select one)*

- **Yes**: LVAD is in an appropriate position  
- **No**: LVAD is not in an appropriate position  

### Location—`OIFMA_MGB_661950`

Indicates the location of the LVAD  
*(Select one)*

- **Intracorporeal**: LVAD is located within the body  
- **Extracorporeal**: LVAD is located outside the body  

### Outflow conduit location—`OIFMA_MGB_023343`

Indicates the location of the outflow conduit of the LVAD  
*(Select one)*

- **Ascending aorta**: Outflow conduit is connected to the ascending aorta  
- **Descending aorta**: Outflow conduit is connected to the descending aorta
