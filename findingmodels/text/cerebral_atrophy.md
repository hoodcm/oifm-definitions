# Cerebral atrophy—`OIFM_OIDM_786384`

**Synonyms:** cerebral volume loss, global cerebral atrophy, brain atrophy, diffuse cerebral atrophy, cerebral parenchymal loss

Cerebral atrophy denotes loss of cerebral parenchymal volume, often diffuse but can be regional. The original phrase provided, "cerebral volume loss", is a commonly used radiology descriptor for this finding.
