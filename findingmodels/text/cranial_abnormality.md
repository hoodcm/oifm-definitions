# Cranial abnormality—`OIFM_OIDM_309677`

**Synonyms:** skull abnormality, abnormal skull contour, cranial deformity, cranial malformation

Cranial abnormality denotes any deviation from normal skull morphology evident on radiologic imaging. This umbrella term encompasses a range of etiologies (congenital, traumatic, infectious, developmental, or post-surgical changes); the original phrasing 'skull abnormality' may be used as an example of this finding.
