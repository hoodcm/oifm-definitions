# Epidural spinal cord compression scale (escc)—`OIFM_CDE_000288`

Epidural Spinal Cord Compression Scale (ESCC) 

**Codes:** RADELEMENT RDES288 epidural spinal cord compression scale (escc)

## Attributes

### Compression grade—`OIFMA_CDE_001998`

Element details for compression grade.  Measured on axial T2-W images at level of greatest compression.  
*(Select one)*

- **grade 0**: Osseous disease only  
- **grade 1a**: Epidural involvement without thecal sac deformation  
- **grade 1b**: Thecal sac deformation without cord contact  
- **grade 1c**: Thecal sac deformation with cord contact  
- **grade 2**: Cord compression with preservation of some CSF  
- **grade 3**: Cord compression with complete effacement of CSF
