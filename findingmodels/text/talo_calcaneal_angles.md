# Talo calcaneal angles—`OIFM_CDE_000200`

Radiographic Evaluation of Congenital and Aqcuired Foot Deformities Talo Calcaneal Angles

**Codes:** RADELEMENT RDES200 talo calcaneal angles

## Attributes

### Ap talo-calcaneal angle	—`OIFMA_CDE_001329`

The angle between the lines drawn down the axis of the talus and the calcaneus on weight-bearing AP foot radiograph.  
Mininum: 0  
Maximum: 180  
Unit: degrees

### Lateral talo-calcaneal angle	—`OIFMA_CDE_001330`

The angle formed by the intersection of the line bisecting the talus through the body and through the neck and the line along the axis of the plantar border of the calcaneus (or bisecting the calcaneus) on lateral weight-bearing views.

  
Mininum: 0  
Maximum: 180  
Unit: degrees

### Ap angle interpretation	—`OIFMA_CDE_001331`
