### Lesion dynamic contrast enhancement (dce) —`OIFMA_CDE_001546`

*(Select one)*

- **DCE negative**: no early or contemporaneous enhancement; or diffuse multifocal enhancement NOT corresponding to a focal finding on T2W and/or DWI or focal enhancement corresponding to a lesion demonstrating features of BPH on T2WI  
- **DCE positive**: Focal, and; earlier than or contemporaneously with enhancement of adjacent normal prostatic tissues, and; corresponds to suspicious finding on T2W and/or DWI
