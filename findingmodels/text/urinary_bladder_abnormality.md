# Urinary bladder abnormality—`OIFM_OIDM_437042`

**Synonyms:** bladder abnormality, urinary bladder abnormality, bladder contour abnormality, abnormality of the urinary bladder

Urinary bladder abnormality is a non-specific radiologic descriptor for any irregularity of the bladder identified on imaging, including contour irregularities, wall thickening, intraluminal masses, or calcifications. Correlation with clinical history and, if indicated, additional targeted imaging or endoscopic evaluation may be required to define etiology.
