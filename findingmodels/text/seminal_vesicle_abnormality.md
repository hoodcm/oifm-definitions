# Seminal vesicle abnormality—`OIFM_OIDM_452510`

**Synonyms:** seminal vesicle abnormality, seminal vesicle pathology, seminal vesicle lesion, seminal vesicle anomaly, SV abnormality, seminal vesicle cyst, seminal vesicle mass, congenital seminal vesicle anomaly

Seminal vesicle abnormality denotes any structural abnormality of the seminal vesicles, including congenital anomalies, cystic dilation, mass lesions, or calcifications. Radiologic evaluation should document size, morphology, symmetry, and any communication with the ejaculatory ducts, with attention to unilateral versus bilateral involvement and to associated findings such as ejaculatory duct obstruction.
