# Hip subsidence—`OIFM_CDE_000062`

This module describes the common data elements for hip subsidence use case

**Codes:** RADELEMENT RDES62 hip subsidence; RADLEX RID2639 hip

## Attributes

### Measurement—`OIFMA_CDE_000299`

Measure the  amount of  subsidence. Alert if  subsidence  is greater than or equal to 10 mm

Maximum: 30
