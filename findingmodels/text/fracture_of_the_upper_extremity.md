# Fracture of the upper extremity—`OIFM_OIDM_971596`

**Synonyms:** upper extremity fracture, upper limb fracture, fracture of the upper limb, UE fracture

Fracture of the upper extremity is a disruption of cortical continuity in any bone of the upper limb, from the shoulder girdle to the distal hand. Radiographic findings may include a fracture line with displacement, angulation, or rotation, and may be associated with dislocation or soft tissue injury depending on the location; this canonical name generalizes the original phrasing 'upper extremity fracture' as the example use case.
