# Glasgow coma scale (gcs)—`OIFM_CDE_000006`

The Glasgow Coma Scale (GCS) is a standardized instrument for assessing the level of consciousness. It evaluates three aspects of responsiveness: eye opening, motor response, verbal response. From the National Institute of Neurological Disorders and Stroke (NIH / NINDS).
