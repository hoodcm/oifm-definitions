# Prostatic abnormality—`OIFM_OIDM_332518`

**Synonyms:** prostate abnormality, prostatic lesion, prostate lesion, prostate gland abnormality, abnormality of the prostate

Prostatic abnormality refers to any deviation from normal prostate gland parenchyma on imaging. It is a non-specific descriptor that may reflect benign etiologies such as benign prostatic hyperplasia or inflammatory processes, or malignant disease. Further characterization with dedicated imaging and clinical correlation is typically required to define etiology.
