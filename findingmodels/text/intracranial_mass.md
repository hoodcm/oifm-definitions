# Intracranial mass—`OIFM_OIDM_119725`

**Synonyms:** intracranial mass, brain mass, intracranial lesion, cranial mass, intracerebral mass, intracranial tumor

A focal space-occupying lesion within the cranial cavity that may reflect neoplasm, hemorrhage, infection, or other pathology; may cause edema or mass effect depending on size and location. The original phrasing 'intracranial mass' is provided as an example use case.
