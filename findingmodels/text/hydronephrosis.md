# Hydronephrosis—`OIFM_OIDM_874812`

**Synonyms:** hydronephrosis, renal pelvis dilation, renal pelvis dilatation, dilated renal pelvis, collecting system dilation

Hydronephrosis refers to dilation of the renal pelvis and calyces caused by obstruction to urinary outflow distal to the kidney. It may be unilateral or bilateral; chronic obstruction can lead to cortical thinning and impaired renal function.
