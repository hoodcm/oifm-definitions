# Colonic diverticulosis—`OIFM_OIDM_388787`

**Synonyms:** colonic diverticulosis, diverticulosis of the colon, colonic diverticula, diverticular disease of the colon

Presence of multiple diverticula within the colon, most commonly in the sigmoid colon, seen as outpouchings along the colonic wall. It is typically an incidental finding and does not show signs of acute inflammation unless diverticulitis is present.
