# Coronary stent—`OIFM_MGB_177589`

A coronary stent is a small mesh-like tube that is placed in a narrowed or blocked coronary artery to restore blood flow.

## Attributes

### Presence of coronary stent—`OIFMA_MGB_738634`

Indicates whether a coronary stent is present or absent.  
*(Select one)*

- **Present**: A coronary stent is present.  
- **Absent**: A coronary stent is not present.  

### Status—`OIFMA_MGB_825426`

Indicates the status of the coronary stent.  
*(Select one)*

- **New placement**: The coronary stent has been newly placed.  
- **Unchanged**: The coronary stent remains unchanged.  
- **Complication**: There is a complication associated with the coronary stent.  
- **Removed**: The coronary stent has been removed.  

### Location—`OIFMA_MGB_002313`

Indicates the location of the coronary stent.  
*(Select one)*

- **Left coronary**: The coronary stent is located in the left coronary artery.  
- **Left anterior descending (LAD)**: The coronary stent is located in the left anterior descending artery.  
- **Left circumflex (LCx)**: The coronary stent is located in the left circumflex artery.  
- **Left obtuse marginal (OM)**: The coronary stent is located in the left obtuse marginal artery.  
- **Right coronary (RCA)**: The coronary stent is located in the right coronary artery.  
- **Right acute marginal**: The coronary stent is located in the right acute marginal artery.
